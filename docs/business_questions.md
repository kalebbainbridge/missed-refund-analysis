# Business Questions

## Monthly Operations

- How many Debt Held cases appear in each monthly snapshot?
- What is the total and average outstanding balance?
- How many cases are Actioned, marked No Action or Raised?
- Which refund types account for the highest case volumes and financial values?

## Customer Outcomes

- How many customers contacted the business before their case was reviewed?
- Which cases may require greater priority due to customer contact or sensitive cancellation circumstances?

## Quality and Training

- Which departments account for the highest volume of missed-refund cases?
- Which agents are associated with the most cases?
- What are the most common root causes?
- Did the simulated training intervention reduce payment-date misunderstandings?
- Did the simulated new-starter period affect the earlier improvement?

## Weekly Operational Reporting

- How does the unresolved backlog change each week?
- When does the backlog reach its highest level?
- How old are cases when they are reviewed or completed?
- How does variable processing capacity affect backlog growth and reduction?
- How is completed work distributed between trained agents?
- How many cases require senior review or another department?

## Process Improvement

- How could the manual import and tracking process be improved?
- How could case prioritisation support better customer outcomes?
- What additional data would be required to calculate departmental error rates and service-level performance?

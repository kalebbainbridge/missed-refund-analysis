# Business Profile

## Overview

This synthetic business represents a UK pet insurance provider that reviews outstanding customer balances each month through a process known as Debt Held.

A monthly Power BI snapshot identifies policies with outstanding balances that require investigation.

The report is exported into an operational Excel workbook where agents manually review each case.

---

# Operational Assumptions

## Monthly Snapshot

- Snapshot produced once per month
- Workbook is usually reviewed around the middle of the following month

## Departments
The synthetic business contains four operational departments that can process policy cancellations.
- Customer Service
- Retentions
- Claims
- Complaints

These departments are responsible for cancelling policies and raising refunds where applicable.
A small group of trained Debt Held agents review the cases.
## Refund Values

Typical refund values:

- Minimum: £0.50
- Usually: £10–£100
- Maximum: Around £300

## Process

Cases are reviewed individually.

Each case is either:

- Actioned
- Marked No Action
- Raised

Comments are added where appropriate.
